## A Cat

A small, furry carnivore.

Domesticated approximately 10,000 years ago.

Currently in charge of the internet.

### Characteristics

- Four legs (usually)
- Tail (mostly)
- Ears (pointy)
- Attitude (always)

### Behavior

Cats sleep 16 hours per day.

The remaining 8 hours are spent:
- Demanding food
- Ignoring you
- Knocking things off tables
- Judging

### Scientific Classification

*Felis catus*

### Conclusion

You don't own a cat.

A cat tolerates your existence.
