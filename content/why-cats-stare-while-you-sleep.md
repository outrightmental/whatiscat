## Possible reasons:

### Protection
You are unconscious. The cat is awake. Someone must keep watch.

### Bond Monitoring
Cats check on members of their social group.

### Curiosity
You make strange noises. Your breathing changes. You twitch.

### Ownership Audit
Just confirming you're still there.
